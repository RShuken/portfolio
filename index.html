About me

I'm a passionate self learner with the scars to prove it. I grew up in the US, but I've spent half of my life living in Shanghai, China. It was difficult the first few years but now I have two homes, Santa Barbara and Shanghai! I was caught up in the tail wind of a startup for many years, then fell into venture capital for the last 8 years. Now I'm on a mission to design my own future and coding is what I want to do! I've seen the incredible progress made thorugh the hard work of coders bringing ideas into reality. I want to learn how to join the ranks of developers and work my way up to building AIs.

Hobbies
